ALTER ROLE

Use this command to change the authorization needed to enable a role. 

 ALTER ROLE role
   { NOT IDENTIFIED
   | IDENTIFIED {BY password | EXTERNALLY | GLOBALLY} }

See also: CREATE ROLE , DROP ROLE , GRANT (Object Privileges) , GRANT (System Privileges and Roles) , SET ROLE
